    Title: Neon Requiem
    Genre: Cyberpunk, Sci-Fi, Action, Thriller
    Episodes: 14
    Studio: AetherEdge Animation

    In the neon-drenched megacity of Elysion, where corporate AI run the streets and memories are commodities, a rogue data-runner named Kira Voss is pulled into a deadly conspiracy after a high-stakes job goes sideways. Hunted by assassins, bounty hunters, and a mysterious entity known only as The Architect, Kira must unravel a hidden truth buried deep within the city’s neural network—a truth that could bring down the entire synthetic order. With pulse-pounding action, mind-bending tech, and a haunting synth soundtrack, Neon Requiem is a stylish descent into a world where humanity flickers like a dying signal, and salvation might just lie in the ghost of a machine.
